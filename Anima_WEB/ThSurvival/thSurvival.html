The survival

A contemporary series comprising seven short animations depicts the trajectory of a being "He/She or thing," from birth to adulthood.

Throughout this journey, the entity accumulates, inhales, and digests knowledge within the environment it calls home.

However, upon being displaced and entering a new environment, which the entity perceives as a safe world, this unfamiliar setting annihilates all its accumulated knowledge, experiences, and essence. To an extent, the subject loses its sense of personality and existence in the face of the transformative impact of the new surroundings. Read More
