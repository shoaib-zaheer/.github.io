60 million education

A group of honourable people from far away paid over 60 million papers for my education.

Long before my interference with the human world, my future was decided by a group of honorable people. They determined how I may live, what I should study, and what my future form and format would be. This project embarks on a journey to navigate between distinguished paradoxes. where the subject of this project was. The gnomon of time allied in an enforced certainty, while the conclusion to manufacture object and objective hung in a pendulum of a colorless black hole.


Anonymity is supposed to be that: In 1970 USA started an education project for Afghanistan children, while simultaneously, the USSR began to sightsee Afghanistan with its chain and roar.
Yet the subject traveled with his naive imagination and ambiguous locus through millions of abstract dimensions to present you with this project. Now, in the present, you will travel in these little footprints to seek what you heard and what it is. Wave the threads of uncertain dimension for yourself.
